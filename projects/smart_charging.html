Smart Charging Infrastructure Planning Tool


SCRIPT serves two main goals: 1) to propose and implement a daily predictive smart charging strategy that foresees the future travel needs of drivers and grid conditions; 2) to study the role of different charging infrastructure investment strategies in the effectiveness of EV smart charging techniques to accommodate state level renewable penetration goals in CA.
